Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies on the collection and use of information when you use Passtation. Unlike traditional services, Passtation does not collect, store, or share your personal data.

1. No Collection of Personal Data

We do not ask you to provide personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or phone number. There is no account registration required to use the Service.

2. Local Storage and Data Persistence

All data you "save" within the application (such as passwords in your vault) is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's localStorage.

  • We cannot access this data.
  • We do not transmit this data to any server.
  • Your data remains on your device until you manually delete it or clear your browser's cache.

3. Password Generation Security

Passtation uses the Web Crypto API to generate passwords locally on your machine. This ensures that your passwords are created using cryptographically secure methods without ever leaving your browser environment.

4. Tracking and Cookies

Passtation does not use tracking cookies, web beacons, or third-party analytics (such as Google Analytics) to monitor your behavior.

5. Links to Other Websites

Our Service contains links to other sites (such as GitHub). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site's privacy policy. We have no control over the practices of third-party sites.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us
